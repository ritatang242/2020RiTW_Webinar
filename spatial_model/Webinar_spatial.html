Spatial Model expression

    1. The value of Xs in a region might affect (or be related to ) the value of y in a neighboring region (lag X)

\[\mathrm Y= X\beta+WX\theta+\varepsilon\]

Make the weights matrix

\[ W= \left[ \begin{matrix} W_{11} & W_{12} & \cdots & W_{1n} \\ W_{21} & W_{22} & \cdots & W_{2n} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ W_{n1} & W_{n2} & \cdots & W_{nn} \\ \end{matrix} \right] \]

KK <- select(K,all_of(n));KK$Delta <- abs(KK$Delta); KK <- na.omit(KK)
queen.nb=poly2nb(KK)
queen.listw=nb2listw(queen.nb)
listw1= queen.listw
